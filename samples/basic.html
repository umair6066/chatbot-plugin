Basic setup

The chat button appears in the bottom-right corner. Click it to open the widget.

Try typing: "what products do you have?"  ·  "tell me about the headphones"  ·  "what's cheapest?"  ·  "wireles headfone" (typo — it still matches!)

Smart suggestion chips

The widget shows clickable chips below each bot reply. The welcome message always has quick-start chips. If the bot doesn't understand a query it shows fallback chips so users always know what to ask next.

What products do you have? What's in stock? Show me prices Help me find something

Chips on older messages grey out — only the latest reply has active chips.

Custom welcome suggestions

Pass a suggestions array to replace the auto-generated chips:

<script src="./chatbot-widget.iife.js"></script>
<script>
  ChatbotWidget.init({
    title: "Support Chat",
    welcomeMessage: "Hi there! 👋 How can I help?",
    suggestions: [
      "What products do you have?",
      "What's your return policy?",
      "Do you ship internationally?",
      "Talk to a human",
    ],
  });
</script>

If suggestions is omitted the widget auto-generates chips from your product catalog.

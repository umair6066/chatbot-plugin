Custom suggestion chips

Suggestions are clickable quick-reply chips shown below the bot's messages. They guide users and auto-update based on context after each reply.

Open the chat and click any chip — it fires as a user message immediately. Then notice new context-aware chips appear after each reply. Older chips grey out.

How suggestions work

Welcome message chips always shown

What products do you have? What's in stock? Show me prices Tell me about Rolex, Submariner Date…

Auto-generated from your product catalog. Pass a suggestions array to override.

After a product listing reply

Tell me about Rolex, Submariner Date, 116610LN, 2019, Steel Tell me about Omega, Seamaster 300M… Tell me about Rolex, Daytona…

Context-aware — chips reflect the products just shown.

When query is unclear — fallback chips

What products do you have? What's in stock? Show me prices Tell me about Rolex, Submariner Date…

When the bot says "I'm not sure I understood…" these chips rescue the conversation.

1 — Auto-generated (default)

<script src="./chatbot-widget.iife.js"></script>
<script>
  ChatbotWidget.init({
    products: [ /* your catalog */ ],
    // No suggestions key — auto-built from products
  });
</script>

2 — Custom welcome chips

<script src="./chatbot-widget.iife.js"></script>
<script>
  ChatbotWidget.init({
    suggestions: [
      "Search Rolex",
      "Search by ref number",
      "What's in stock?",
      "Show me prices",
    ],
  });
</script>

3 — Business-specific chips (no products)

<script src="./chatbot-widget.iife.js"></script>
<script>
  ChatbotWidget.init({
    title: "Support",
    suggestions: [
      "I have a billing question",
      "My order hasn't arrived",
      "I want to return something",
      "Talk to a human",
    ],
  });
</script>

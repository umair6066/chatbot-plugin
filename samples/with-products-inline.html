Products — inline array

Pass products directly inside ChatbotWidget.init(). Good when the data is small or already available in your page.

Try asking: "what products do you have?"  ·  "tell me about the headphones"  ·  "what's in stock?"  ·  "show prices"  ·  "cheapest option"  ·  "show electronics"

Auto-generated suggestion chips

Because products are passed inline, the widget immediately builds product-aware chips for the welcome message. After each reply you also get context-specific follow-up chips.

What products do you have? What's in stock? Show me prices Tell me about Wireless Headphones

These are generated automatically — no extra config needed.

Custom suggestions example

<script>
  ChatbotWidget.init({
    products: [ /* your catalog */ ],
    suggestions: [
      "What products do you have?",
      "What's your cheapest product?",
      "Show Electronics",
      "What's out of stock?",
    ],
  });
</script>
